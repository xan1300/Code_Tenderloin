ALL ABOUT EMPEROR PENGUIN

Emperor Penguins are the biggest penguins out of all living penguin species. This species is also found in Anarctica. The male and female are similar in size reaching heights of 3.6-4.3 feet tall and weighing 22 to 45 kg. These penguins have a lifespan of 20 years. They have a stable population.

WHAT PENGUINS EAT

  • Antarctic Silverfish
  • Krill
  • Squid

STEPS TO SEE PENGUINS

SIMPLE WAY

  1. Save up a lot of money
  2. Plan a trip to Anarctica
  3. Make sure you have lots of warm clothes
  4. Spend time traveling there
  5. Once in Anarctica find a near by group of Penguins
  6. After months of prep watch the penguins from a distance to not disturb them
DIFFICULT WAY

  1. Search for your local zoo
  2. Check if they have Penguins
  3. Plan a day to go
  4. Travel to the zoo
  5. Watch the penguins
