ALL ABOUT PORCUPINES

The North American Porcupine is a large quill-covered rodent. This is the second largest rodent after the North American Beaver. Porcupines are not a very aggressive animal and will only attack if threatened. This Porcupine can have 30,000 quills that are all very sharp.

PORCUPINE PREDATORS

  • Great Horned Owl
  • Bobcat
  • Fisher
  • Cougar
  • Coyote
  • Wolf
  • Wolverine
  • Lynxes
  • Canada Lynx

WHAT TO DO IF YOU GET QUILLED

  1. Cut ends of quills to release the pressure from the quill
  2. Grab some pliers or something to pull them out
  3. Grab the quills with the pliers
  4. Twist and pull straight up
  5. The quill should slide out of your skin
  6. Finally disinfect the area and call a doctor
