ALL ABOUT GOLDEN RETRIEVERS

Golden Retrievers are a medium to large dog. They were originally bred to retrieve game such as ducks while hunting. They got the name “retriever” because of their soft mouths which allowed them to retrieve shot game without damaging it.

FUN FACTS

  • Life span: 10-12 year
  • Male weight: 65-75 lbs
  • Female weight: 55-71 lbs
  • Male height: 22-24 inches
  • Female height: 20-22 inches

TRAIN A GOLDEN RETRIEVER TO SIT

  1. Hold a treat infront of the dogs nose
  2. Lift the treat over the dogs head so they look up
  3. Wait for the dog to naturally sit back to follow the treat
  4. Once the dog sits give the command "sit"
  5. Give praise and the treat
  6. Practice and switch out the teach with a hand signal
